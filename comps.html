About Department

Ramrao Adik Institute of Technology (RAIT) has evolved into one of the premier institutes for technical education of the Dr. D.Y.PATIL GROUP. The computer engineering department has been a part of RAIT, since it was established in 1983 with it’s under graduate intake capacity of 120 students. It’s been a great pride to state that, RAIT was among the very few institutes have computer engineering department since its inception in year 1983.With an objective to impart quality education in computer engineering field that can equip the budding engineers with the right knowledge and propel them to the path of success in the country and abroad. The long term goal of the department is to harness the skills of the faculty and students to create a technically sound learning environment that can be beneficial to industry, society and research innovations. The National Board of Accreditation (NBA), New Delhi, has accredited the department for three years in 2005. The Department is conducting the post graduation (PG) program i.e. Master of Engineering (ME) affiliated to University of Mumbai since 2005 with a capacity of 18 seats which increased to 36 in 2012. A Doctoral Program (PhD) with a capacity of 10 students is running since 2012. The under graduate intake is increased to 180 in 2013.
