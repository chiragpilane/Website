About Department

The department of Information Technology was started in the year 1999, to coincide with the need to produce efficient and effective engineers for satisfying the immense requirement for professionals in the field of Information Technology. With the aim to produce smart I.T. professionals and researchers, the Masters of Information Technology was started in the year 2012. The department aims to provide the students with the platform to develop insight in the current IT trends/problems and motivates them to develop innovative solutions.
